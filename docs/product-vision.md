# Product Vision

Create a premium World Cup 2026 sweepstake app that feels fast, simple, social, polished, and excellent on mobile.

## Audience

Office teams, friendship groups, families, clubs, and communities that want a low-friction World Cup sweepstake without spreadsheets or manual updates.

## Experience Principles

- Setup should feel effortless for admins.
- Participants should not need accounts to view a shared sweepstake link unless privacy settings require it.
- Team allocation should feel fair, transparent, and exciting.
- Leaderboards, badges, and team progress should be easy to scan.
- Football data should feel reliable even when free-tier scores are delayed.
- AI should add summaries and banter without becoming the product.

## Core Product Loop

1. Admin creates a sweepstake.
2. Admin adds participants.
3. Admin randomly allocates all 48 teams fairly.
4. Participants open a shared link to see their teams.
5. Cached football data updates team points, badge status, and leaderboard positions.
6. Optional AI and email updates keep the group engaged.

## Differentiation

The app replaces messy office sweepstake spreadsheets with a premium mobile experience: fair allocation, automatic tournament tracking, badge visibility, and optional AI-powered summaries.
