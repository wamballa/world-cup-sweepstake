# Customer Journey

## Admin Journey

1. Create a new sweepstake.
2. Enter the sweepstake name.
3. Select FIFA World Cup 2026.
4. Enter participant names.
5. Add optional participant email addresses.
6. Add other admins if needed.
7. Press `Randomly Allocate Teams`.
8. Review fair allocation of 48 teams across participants.
9. Share the scoreboard link.
10. Track teams, points, badges, and leaderboard movement during the tournament.

## Participant Journey

1. Receive shared sweepstake link.
2. See allocated team or teams.
3. Optionally add an email address for updates.
4. View the participant leaderboard.
5. Follow matches, team progress, badge status, points changes, and stats.
6. Receive optional email updates for points changes, match results, and badge changes.

## Low-Friction Principle

Participants should be able to understand their teams, rank, badges, and recent changes within seconds of opening the link.
